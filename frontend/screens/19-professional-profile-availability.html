About Dr. Jenkins

Dr. Sarah Jenkins is a pioneering neurologist with over 15 years of dedicated experience in treating complex spinal disorders and neurological conditions. As the Chief Neurologist at Johns Hopkins Hospital, she leads a team of specialists focused on minimally invasive surgical techniques that prioritize patient recovery and long-term wellness.

Her approach combines cutting-edge medical technology with a deeply personalized care philosophy. Dr. Jenkins believes that understanding the patient's lifestyle and goals is just as critical as the diagnosis itself. She has published over 40 peer-reviewed papers on neuroplasticity and chronic pain management.
